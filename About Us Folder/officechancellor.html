Office Of The Chancellor

The Chancellor

High Chief Dr. (Mrs.) Winifred Awosika, OON

High Chief Dr. (Mrs.) Winifred Awosika, OON, is the esteemed Chancellor and Founder of Chrisland University. A visionary educationist, she has dedicated her life to fostering academic excellence and character development in Nigeria’s education sector. Her journey in education began with the establishment of Chrisland Schools Limited, a network of schools known for providing high-quality education at various levels. With a passion for holistic learning, she expanded this vision by founding Chrisland University in 2015, furthering her commitment to higher education and innovation (Chrisland University). Dr. Awosika’s contributions to education have earned her national and international recognition, including the prestigious Officer of the Order of the Niger (OON) award. Her leadership has not only shaped the growth of Chrisland University but has also influenced Nigeria’s educational landscape through policies that emphasize academic excellence, ethical standards, and global competitiveness.

Office of the Chancellor

The Office of the Chancellor is at the highest level of the university’s administrative and academic structure. It oversees all functional areas of the institution, ensuring the effective management of both academic and non-academic activities in alignment with the university’s vision and goals. The office provides leadership and strategic direction, working to enhance institutional growth and excellence. History of the University

The University commenced with two Colleges- the College of Arts, Management, and Social Sciences, and the College of Natural and Applied Sciences. The College of Basic Medical Sciences became the third college in 2019, while the College of Law received NUC Accreditation in 2022. The incumbent Vice-Chancellor of the Institution, Prof. Mrs. Chinedum Peace Babalola, FAS, championed full accreditation of nine (9) programmes (Business Administration, Economics, Accounting, Criminology & Security Studies, Psychology, Computer Science, Political Science, Microbiology and Biochemistry) presented for the 2018 NUC Accreditation. Three programmes (Mass Communication, Banking and Finance, and International Relations) which were presented for NUC accreditation in 2021 got full accreditation also in 2021.
