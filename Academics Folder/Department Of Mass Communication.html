Department Of Mass Communication

About Mass Communication

The Department of Mass Communication at Chrisland University is dedicated to shaping skilled and ethical media professionals equipped to thrive in the dynamic world of communication. The program provides a comprehensive education in journalism, broadcasting, public relations, advertising, digital media, and corporate communication. With a blend of theoretical knowledge and hands-on practical training, students gain expertise in news writing, media production, content creation, and strategic communication. The department emphasizes creativity, critical thinking, and ethical reporting, preparing graduates for careers in television, radio, print media, online media, public relations, and corporate communication. Through modern facilities, industry exposure, and interactive learning, students are equipped to excel in the ever-evolving media landscape.

CAMAS Dean

Prof. Benjamin O. Olley

The College of Arts, Management, and Social Sciences (CAMASS) at Chrisland University is led by Professor Benjamin O. Olley, an esteemed academic with extensive experience in the fields of management and social sciences. Under his leadership, CAMASS offers a diverse range of programs designed to equip students with the knowledge and skills necessary to excel in various professional domains. The college emphasizes a comprehensive understanding of human behavior, societal dynamics, and effective management practices, preparing graduates to contribute meaningfully to societal development.
