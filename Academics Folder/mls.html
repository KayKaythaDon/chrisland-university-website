About MEDICAL LABORATORY SCIENCE

The Department of Medical Laboratory Science at Chrisland University provides students with a comprehensive education in laboratory diagnostics, medical technology, and clinical research. The program covers essential areas such as hematology, microbiology, biochemistry, immunology, pathology, and blood transfusion science, preparing students to excel in the field of medical diagnostics. With hands-on training in state-of-the-art laboratory facilities, students develop practical skills in diagnostic testing, quality control, and medical research. The department emphasizes ethical practice, critical thinking, and scientific inquiry, ensuring that graduates are well-equipped to provide accurate and timely diagnostic services in hospitals, research institutions, public health organizations, and private laboratories. Students also have opportunities for clinical placements, further bridging the gap between theoretical learning and real-world medical practice.

B.Sc. Medical Laboratory Science

Requirements: Maths, Englsih , Commas, Economics , Accounting

Dean Of COBAMS

Prof. Augustine Onyeaghala

The College of Arts, Management, and Social Sciences (COBAMS) at Chrisland University is led by, Prof. Augustine Onyeaghala an esteemed academic with extensive experience in the fields of management and social sciences. Under his leadership, COBAMS offers a diverse range of programs designed to equip students with the knowledge and skills necessary to excel in various professional domains. The college emphasizes a comprehensive understanding of human behavior, societal dynamics, and effective management practices, preparing graduates to contribute meaningfully to societal development.
