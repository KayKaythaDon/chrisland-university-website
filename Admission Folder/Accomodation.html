Accomodation

About Accomodation

Chrisland University provides on-campus accommodation as part of the school tuition, ensuring a comfortable and secure living environment for students. The boys' hostel features rooms that accommodate six students, with each room having its own toilet and bathroom for convenience. The university's accommodation facilities are designed to promote a conducive atmosphere for learning and social interaction, with essential amenities provided to enhance students' comfort during their stay.

About Girls Hostel

The girls' hostel at Chrisland University provides a safe, serene, and well-equipped accommodation facility tailored to support students' academic and personal well-being. Designed for comfort, the hostel offers spacious rooms, a hygienic environment, and essential amenities to ensure a home-like experience. The hostel is secured with round-the-clock surveillance and dedicated staff to address students' needs. Common areas encourage bonding and relaxation, while the peaceful atmosphere creates the perfect balance between study and social life. With reliable power supply and a clean, well-maintained space, students can focus on their education in a supportive and comfortable setting.

About Boys Hostel

The boys' hostel at Chrisland University offers a comfortable and secure living space, fostering a strong sense of camaraderie among students. Each room accommodates six students and comes with an attached toilet and bathroom for convenience and privacy. The hostel is designed to promote both academic focus and social interaction, with study-friendly environments, common areas for relaxation, and essential amenities to make campus life enjoyable. With 24/7 security, power supply, and well-maintained facilities, students can enjoy a stress-free stay while focusing on their studies and personal growth.
