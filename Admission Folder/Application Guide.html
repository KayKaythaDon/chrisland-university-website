Application Guide

What you need to know

Chrisland University offers admission into various undergraduate and postgraduate programs across multiple disciplines. The admission process is designed to be seamless and straightforward, ensuring that all prospective students meet the necessary academic and ethical requirements. Before applying, ensure you meet the general admission requirements, which include: A minimum of five (5) credit passes in relevant subjects, including Mathematics and English, in not more than two sittings (WAEC, NECO, or equivalent). A valid UTME (JAMB) score that meets the university’s cut-off mark. Additional departmental or faculty requirements, where applicable. Step 1:Obtain the Admission Form Visit the Chrisland University Admission Portal to access the online application form. Fill in your details correctly and upload the necessary documents, including academic transcripts and personal information. Alternatively, prospective students can visit the university’s admission office to obtain a physical application form. Step 2: Pay the Application Fee Proceed to make the required application fee payment through the university’s designated payment channels. Ensure you keep a copy of the payment receipt for reference. Step 3: Submit Your Application Carefully review your application to confirm that all details and documents are correctly uploaded. Submit the completed application form via the online portal or directly at the admission office. Step 4: Attend the Screening Exercise After submitting your application, you will be invited for a screening or entrance examination, depending on your program. The screening process may include document verification, an interview, or an entrance test. Step 5 : Complete Registration and Clearance Visit the university campus to complete your course registration, medical screening, and biometric verification. Submit all necessary documents, including proof of payment, academic records, and identification documents.
