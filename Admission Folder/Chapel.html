About the Chapel

The school chapel at Chrisland University serves as a spiritual hub where students, staff, and faculty come together for worship, fellowship, and personal reflection. It provides a serene environment for prayer, counseling, and various religious activities that foster moral and spiritual growth. Regular services, inspirational sermons, and special programs are held to encourage faith, character development, and a strong sense of community. Open to all students, the chapel plays a vital role in promoting ethical values, unity, and a well-rounded university experience.

The Registrar

Mr. Samuel Omotoso

Mr. Samuel Omotoso is the Registrar of Chrisland University, a position he has held since April 2022. With nearly three decades of experience in university administration, he has played a key role in shaping academic and administrative policies across various institutions (Tribune Online). Before joining Chrisland University, Mr. Omotoso served in administrative capacities at Bowen University and Dominion University, where he contributed to the development of efficient institutional frameworks (Independent.ng). His leadership and expertise in educational management have earned him recognition in the field of higher education. He holds a Higher National Diploma (HND) in Secretarial Studies from The Polytechnic, Ibadan. Furthering his education, he obtained a Postgraduate Diploma in Mass Communication and a Master’s Degree in Industrial and Labour Relations from the University of Ibadan (Tribune Online). As a seasoned administrator, Mr. Omotoso is dedicated to fostering excellence, innovation, and a conducive learning environment at Chrisland University. His wealth of experience continues to enhance the university’s commitment to academic growth and institutional efficiency.
