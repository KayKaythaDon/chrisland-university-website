About Feeding

At Chrisland University, students have access to a variety of food vendors and cafeterias offering diverse meal options to suit different tastes and preferences. Meals are not included in the school tuition, so students are required to pay separately for their food. Payment can be made directly at the food shops, with some vendors offering cash, card, and digital payment options for convenience. Whether you're looking for local delicacies, fast food, or healthy meals, there are multiple outlets on campus to cater to your needs.
