Tuition Fees

About Tuition Fees

At Chrisland University, the tuition fees cover the cost of academic programs, faculty instruction, and other essential student services. However, feeding is not included in the tuition fee and is to be arranged separately by the students. There are various food outlets on campus, offering a wide range of meal options for students to choose from. In addition to tuition, accommodation fees are also included as part of the overall charges. The university provides well-equipped hostels with comfortable living arrangements, ensuring students have a conducive environment for both academic and personal development. The accommodation facilities are designed to foster a safe, supportive, and vibrant student community.
