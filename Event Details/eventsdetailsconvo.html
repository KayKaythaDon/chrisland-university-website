6TH CONVOCATION CEREMONY

On behalf of the Board of Trustees, Governing Council, Senate, Staff and Students of Chrisland University, We cordially invite you to the 6th convocation ceremony. This momentous occasion will celebrate the achievements of our graduating students as they embark on the next chapter of their journey. The event will feature the conferment of degrees, award of prizes, and a keynote address by a distinguished guest. Join us as we honor excellence, perseverance, and the spirit of academic distinction. Your presence will be highly appreciated as we mark this significant milestone in the history of Chrisland University.
