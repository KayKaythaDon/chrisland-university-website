Chrisland University Appoints New Vice-Chancellor

Students, staff, alumni, and stakeholders—who contributed to the success and vibrancy of our great Institution.

The past year was filled with remarkable achievements and milestones that have further cemented our University’s reputation for excellence. From groundbreaking research and innovative teaching practices to outstanding performances by our students and staff, we have collectively demonstrated resilience, dedication, and an unwavering commitment to our shared vision.

As we step into this new year and chapter, let us continue to aim higher, embrace challenges with determination, and seize opportunities for growth and innovation. Together, we will make 2025 another year of transformative impact, academic excellence, and enduring success.

I encourage each of you to approach the year with renewed enthusiasm, collaboration, and a sense of purpose. The future is bright, and I have no doubt that, as a community, we will achieve even greater heights.

Happy New Year to you and your loved ones! May this year bring health, happiness, and fulfillment in all your endeavors.
With best regards, Professor Oyedunni Sola ARULOGUN Vice Chancellor Chrisland University
